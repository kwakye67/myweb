About Us

We are reputable firm headquartered in Accra providing debt collection services, revenue collection and skip tracing. We operate on a performance-only basis; our motivation is to get your money back in your hands, where it belongs. Accordingly, our motivation is propelled by mutual success and is result oriented. We are industry specialists when it comes to dealing with large claims while offering a nationwide debt recovery services.

Mission

To generate cash for our customers by offering best in class service, nationwide reach and cross-border expertise.

Vision

To be the trade invoice collection services company of choice nationwide, most valued by our customers, partners and employees.

Core Values

Do the right thing, Be honest, respectful to both clients and debtors.Do what we say, Focus on results, Communicate openly, Value training and education, Be industry experts and leaders. Be passionate and determined.

OUR SERVICES

We strive to achieve out-of-court settlements to minimize stress and burden on our clients and ensure a healthy relationship with the debtors in the long-run. We are well experienced in dealing with debtors in an assertive yet ethical manner. We take into account professional, cultural and linguistic sensibilities when dealing with each debtor to make sure that your cash flow is not jeopardized along the way.

Cordial Collection

We help you promote and retain positive working relationships with your customers by providing non-contentious solutions through our cordial commercial debt collection services.

First Party Credit

We provide professional accounts receivable management. We enable you to focus on your core business activities, while helping you maintain confidence in your credit processes.

Immediate Action

We understand that a great deal of cases are urgent and need immediate attention. That is why we offer our fast track debt collection service. This service means we will contact your debtor within 24hrs of us receiving your debtor’s details.
